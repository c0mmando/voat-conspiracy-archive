If there were a helicopter flying with guns the first questions would be why?, the second issue is all scenarios like that require flash suppression for the very reason of reducing the chance of detection.

A military based mission with a pickup on a rooftop in a civilian area would be done by two helicopters at a minimum.
One for the pick up, the other flying overwatch to ensure the landing area is clear and to provide fire support/area suppression.
And all the nav lights would be off and the blades feathered to reduce the noise profile.

If it were a black op, using an air pickup would increase the likelyhood of mission failure, due to being seen, mechanical failure and also time in theater - picking up with a bird can take longer than a few pre-prepared ground options and usually is used when the ground options are not available.
In the context of the obvious conspiracy around the whole shooting, extraction via ground operations would make the most sense as it's pretty easy to get about unnoticed in densely populated areas.
