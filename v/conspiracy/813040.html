I listened to a few more interviews ( 1 , 2 , 3 ) and he sounds very honest and like he knows what he's talking about to me. He is very clearly not some idiot who just attended community college as Wikipedia wants you, so very badly, to believe. There's something else. The story is that he claims he attended the California Institute of Technology and Massachusetts Institute of Technology but there is no record of it. Now, a quick note, there was also no record of his birth at the hospital until George Knapp tracked down the Dr. who gave birth to him (reference to this is in the lie detector video). What I want to know is if he can provide basic information about, say MIT. Can he tell you where the drinking fountains are or the names of any of his professors? It seems like this would lend a lot to his credibility. He actually claims, though, that he does not want people to believe him anymore because he is running a scientific supply company and doesn't want to be known as "the UFO guy" but if you look at his companies website it's clearly not representing itself as a professional business. So is this resurgence of Lazar just another lie and he actually is trying to get attention while pretending he does not want attention so he doesn't have to be grilled about simple things that anyone who went to MIT would know? Or is he really just a guy who doesn't want attention and doesn't want to add more to a story that has made the whole world ridicule him?
