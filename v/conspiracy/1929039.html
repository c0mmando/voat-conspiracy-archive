VictorSteinerDavion 2017-06-13T00:04

It's generated by software that auto-writes the articles based on a larger collection of texts.

The repetition happens because the algorithm, usually based on markov chains, has no semantic understanding of what the text actually says, it just knows the words in a specific sequence achieve a higher score and can sometimes repeat them as a way to meet the minimum score requirements the human operator imposes.

This blog post from 2010 has some details on it.
Pay close attention to facebook feeds, much of the churn there is auto generated.
