I disagree pretty strongly with regards to digital ballots being non legitimate. You could easily conceive of a digital voting systems in which votes a cryptographically signed in a way that the voter can validate his vote but no one else can. With statistics (since the data is open anyone can do this) it can be reasonably proven that not enough extra votes to influence the election were cast. I believe this system would be much more tamper proof than physical ballots.
