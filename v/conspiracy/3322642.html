Full quote from The Modern Jew (1899) By Arnold White:

If the Jew be essentially parasitic in character and habits ; if he can only live by exploiting the vices or preying on the weaknesses of others ; to whom existence is impossible when forced to face life as other men face it, with the strength of his own right arm ; to whom the sweetest of music is the rustling of bank notes and the chinking of coin ; then the conclusion is irresistible that the Jew is not only a bad Russian citizen, but that the race is disqualified from settlement among civilized communities on any portion of the earth's surface. There is no escape from the logic of this position.
