Crypto Journalism - Conspiracy Science ( conspiracy )

submitted 2017-06-02T15:41 by sinclair

I've been thinking about his a lot... We have timelines that end in truth as facts are uncovered from original conspiracy theories. There are different levels of theories, from reasonable (Bernie campaign was robbed) to tin-foil-hat (Russians are hacking the elections).

At some point, these theories gain enough credibility to become accepted truth. Isn't that how "science" works too these days? Can we create a field of study called, "Crypto Journalism/Conspiracy Science"? What would be the rules? How much of the scientific method could we apply?

Quite literally, politics and political party rhetoric are conspiracies. They are groups of people colluding. In many cases, to persuade others in an attempt to gain power over them. See DNC leaks.

The goal is to take the narrative away from conspiracy theory and make it part of something larger and more noble. Thoughts on this?

Conspirology is from Latin: (conspiratio - conspiracy); and Greek: (λογία - logia), is the scientific study of the nature, causes and effects, extent, control, and prevention of conspirational behaviour in both the individual and in society.

Conspirology is an interdisciplinary field in the speculative sciences, drawing especially upon the research of criminology, sociology, politology, psychology and psychiatry, anthropology, history, theology as well as other related disciplines.

1st conspiracy law - If you can think of any conspiracy that may physically exist, it already does. The main problem is to detect, solve and expose it, which is the conspiracy paradox.
