On December 24 YouTube took down my following video where I shared "My Sources for Political and Economic News". I disputed the takedown and won. Further info and links to my sources included. ( conspiracy )

submitted 2019-12-28T15:01 by ellanana

In 2017, as a part of my efforts to decentralize my content away from YouTube due to its blatant censorship, I started live streaming on Twitch as well as uploading all my videos to BitChute.

I have a modest following on YouTube - approximately 30,000 subs and have been uploading there since 2006 - and one of the most common requests that I have had over the years is to share my sources of political and economic news.

As a result of this request, for one of my first live streams ever, I did a live stream on Twitch where I share my sources of Political and Economics News - this was the second full live stream I have ever done so it is a little raw.

On December 24, 2019, just a few days ago, I received a take down notice from YouTube for this video. Here are pics of the take down: https://imgur.com/a/1lYyhC1 ....YouTube gave me the option to dispute the takedown, which I did.

Today, without even notifying me, I found out that YouTube restored the video. The reason I found this out is because at 9:00 AM PST, in just a couple of hours, I will be doing another live stream on Twitch where we plan on watching the video to try and figure out what might have triggered the take down.
