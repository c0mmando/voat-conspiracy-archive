911: Short And Powerful Questions ( conspiracy )

submitted 2016-09-21T21:22 by Zebra237

A 600,000 pound (272,155.44 Kilogram) chunk of steel was severed from the WTC and blown 400 feet (121.92 Meters) into WTC Tower 3. Can you calculate the force that threw a 600,000 pound object 400 feet? Do you honestly believe this force can be explained by exploding jet fuel and office fires?

There was a COMEX vault containing gold and silver bullion in the basement vault four levels down at WTC Tower 4. Were you aware that one billion dollars of gold and silver went missing from that supposedly secure vault the night before 911?

Are you aware that on 9-10-2001 Donald Rumsfeld, the Secretary of Defense, and his Comptroller, rabbi Dov Zakheim admitted that 2.3 trillion dollars was missing from DOD spending and could not be traced?

What do you think the odds were that it was just a coincidence that whatever struck the Pentagon on 911 was aimed directly at the men and women who were attempting to find the missing trillions killing them and stifling attempts to recover the stolen money?

440,000 cubic yards (336,404.14 cubic meters) of reinforced concrete on WTC Towers 1 and 2 were pulverized and turned into dust. Can you honestly believe that much explosive power was generated by jet fuel and office fires?

The North and South Towers had 47 central core steel columns and 236 perimeter columns for a total of 283. For the buildings to fall straight down at nearly free fall speed all 283 connections from the columns to each of the 110 floors had to be cut within a second of each other. Keeping in mind open air fires cannot reach half of the temperature required to melt steel, how do you explain (2 X 110 X 283) 62,260 connections in the two Towers all being severed simultaneously by an office fire? Isn’t this a definition of a controlled demolition?
