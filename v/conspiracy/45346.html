If an internet savvy person was determined to find it, they could likely find pictures of child pornography somewhere deep in the web. And all I would have to do is ask to borrow someone's laptop for a moment, plug in a flash drive, and the beginnings of owning that person are mine. The activity they do is irrelevant, possibly slightly humiliating, it's just something to ingratiate them to me (although I would remain steadfastly anonymous). To make them concede that doing the first task is easier than running to the authorities themselves.

Next it would be a progressive system of more and more illegal activities for this individual. Nothing has to happen fast, I could wait weeks, months, or even years in-between each contact with this specific individual and task required of them--but it would get progressively more illegal in order to snare them further. Probably start with something small "obtain some marijuana, leave it in x bathroom on campus" but done in a way to collect more evidence against the individual (for instance having a camera setup in that bathroom to catch them doing the illegal activity). This would make it to where they are actually complicit in increasingly dangerous/illegal activities in order to increase control of this individual. Only I would know everyone in on it, and all members would have evidence of some other members, but no other member would know about all members besides myself.

My first victim would essentially be my patsy in this regard--if he fails, it's no skin off my teeth and I can begin again. If he succeeds... In this way I could easily create a machine that builds based on fear and anonymity. The only tricky parts that remain are a communication network, and the ability to meet with large segments of important members of the group. With the rise of TOR and the demand for anonymous communication in the wake of the NSA revelations given to us by Snowden, I don't deem this to be an impossible task either though.

It doesn't matter in this way what my ideology was, I could start a secret society based on it like this, and the members would have no say so about it. This would be a game played for the long term, but a patient man could accomplish all of the things I described.

And if I could do it, then so could someone else. And maybe, someone already has. All I know is that these ideas coming up over in Europe about the pedophile-sex-ring-scandals are disturbingly reminiscent of the practices of a secret society, and the public at large doesn't seem to be aware of just how big of a deal those revelations are.
