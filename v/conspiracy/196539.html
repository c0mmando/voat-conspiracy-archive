submitted 2015-07-05T16:12 by l23r

At the end of this Pro-Life ad it says it's not endorsed by a person, party or organization. This is not true.

Fidelis, the group which produced the ad, generated over 1 million dollars last year. It's financially backed by Schwab. Schwab gets money from The New Common School Foundation, which is headed by Republican senator Clark Durant... I found this information after Googling "Fidelis" and going to citizenaudit.org

I don't think this is a conspiracy, but I could see how other people might think this is a game of "connect the dots" while wearing tinfoil hats.
