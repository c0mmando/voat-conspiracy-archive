The problem with studies like this is WHO is defining what "misinformation" is. In this case main stream media was designated as the truthful source, and mostly everything else as fake news.

So the honest name for this study should really be "Anatomy of an anti-establishment network". In this context it could be an interesting read, but otherwise you can almost say the publication itself, based on its title, is fake news :)
