Hi all,

Just joined voat after being fed up with censorship and shadowbans at reddit. The shop is at http://conspira-tees.com/

Going for a mostly playful and fun vibe. The idea is that these shirts are something that will start conversations -- "Hey, what does your shirt mean?" This helps further the causes that mean something to you, and overall, hopefully helps to wake up more people to questioning everything. I'm hoping to add more t-shirts, hoodies, and other conspiracy theory related merchandise.

It's hard for me to claim it's entirely altruistic; obviously, I'm selling wares. If sales go well, my hope is to delve into researching and writing articles about conspiracies in depth. I figure that this way, it's a more authentic article (supported by apparel) vs. one simply trying to grab the most pageviews. Starting short and understandable, and gaining traction and going into more depth as the article progresses.

What topics do you think deserves a shirt? What works, what doesn't? What do you want to see? All feedback is welcomed!
