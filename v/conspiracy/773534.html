I won't say that Tor is untrustworthy, but I will say that you shouldn't use it as your only layer of protection. Using Tor in combination with a VPN, using VMs and full disk encryption, and logging in from a location that is not your home will put you on the right track to actual privacy. That's on top of not running any javascript, using free software, on a machine that can and does run libreboot.

Tor by itself doesn't fulfill all of a user's privacy needs, and that's just a conservative assessment. If you wanna be super paranoid, even Tor can't be trusted. You'd be better running it than not running it, but I wouldn't put all of my faith in it.
