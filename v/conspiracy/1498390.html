The word conspiracy means a criminal undertaking agreed to by two or more individuals. It is a crime.

Lets say you agree with your friend to cook some meth. and buy ingredients.... even if you dont complete your task and you're not guilty of possession of meth, or trafficking... you can still be guilty of conspiracy to manufacture or something like that.

Conspiracy is not a general term for any and all nefarious schemes, it is not a general term for any evil acts that are undereported. (these could be conspiracies if the scheme or undereporting is part of a criminal agreemant).
