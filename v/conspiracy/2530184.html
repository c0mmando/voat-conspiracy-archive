It is a warning of illusion and delusion, a good person who lives in a fool's paradise; a dreamer of dreams who awakens only when surrounded by danger. It is also a number of false judgement owing to the influence of others."

I took the trouble to download Cheiro's Book of Numbers . There seems to be no reason whatsoever, symbolical or otherwise, for doing so, other than that it made it easier for Cheiro to come up with the descriptions to associate with these numbers.
