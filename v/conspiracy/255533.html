Putting an entity with a monopoly on force

  1. Incorrect statement. Nobody has a monopoly on force. The ability to use force varies.

Gross exaggeration. Fairness doctrine only said that, in certain situations, an alternative view must be presented. It did not say at all what could be said or not said, and it surely did not pick the speakers. Nearly all of TV broadcast was completely free of fairness doctrine requirements.
