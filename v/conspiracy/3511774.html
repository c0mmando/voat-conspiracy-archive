Deus Ex Machina ( conspiracy )

submitted 2019-11-11T21:21 by Conspirologist

What Deus Ex Machina really means? According to Latin, it means god from the machine. This first interpretation means the ancient people have witnessed an alien coming out from a spaceship. According to modern english, it means god who is former machine, which can be interpreted as AI that becomes god. It is interesting how both interpretations are following the timeline of the evolution of the human race. An alien can be god among ancestors as AI can be god among descendants.
