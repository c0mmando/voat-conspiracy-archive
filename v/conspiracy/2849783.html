Once awakened, as Jesus was awakened, a man or a woman is more powerful and greater than Yaldabaoth or the lesser Archons who continue to preside over our material realm.

In essence, the Jews are the demons who run hell, and their god is Satan. This is true in a very real sense, it is not just a metaphor. The Jews are serving Yaldabaoth by corrupting the rest of mankind, and their reward is rule over mankind.
